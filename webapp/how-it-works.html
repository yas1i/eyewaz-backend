How EYEWAZ works

Four simple steps from written words to natural sound — no sight required.

Step 1

Snap it — or paste it

Point your camera at a letter, menu, or book page. Or upload a document, paste a web link, or type. Anything written works.

Step 2

EYEWAZ reads it

In seconds, the words become clear, natural speech — read aloud in real time with voices built for the ear.

Hear it your way

Choose your language, dialect, and voice — Urdu, regional Pakistani dialects, and many world languages. Set the speed that suits you.

Step 4

Keep it forever

Save readings into your own library, organise them in folders, and replay them anytime — even offline. Set spoken reminders so nothing is missed.
