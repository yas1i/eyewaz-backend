Empowering Access Through Sound

EYEWAZ is a web-based screen reader designed to make the digital world more inclusive for blind and visually impaired users everywhere. With intuitive navigation, high-quality text-to-speech, and seamless compatibility across websites, it transforms on-screen content into clear, natural audio in real time.

What we do

Built with accessibility at its core — turning barriers into opportunities so the web is truly for everyone.

Read anything aloud

Photograph printed text, upload a document or PDF, paste a link, or type — and hear it spoken.

Natural Urdu & more

Clear, human-sounding voices in Urdu, regional dialects, and many world languages.

Spoken reminders

Medicine, prayers, appointments — EYEWAZ reminds you out loud, at the right time.

Your own library

Save what you read into folders and replay it anytime — even offline.

Works everywhere

Runs in your browser and on your phone — and powers screen readers across the web.

Hands-free

Speak to EYEWAZ and listen back — independent browsing, no sight required.

Who we are

EYEWAZ was built around one belief: information should be for everyone. We are a small team creating tools that turn written words into spoken ones, so people who are blind or have low vision can read, learn, and stay connected — independently and with dignity.
