Get EYEWAZ on your device

EYEWAZ runs everywhere a browser runs — phone, tablet, or computer. Scan once, install to your home screen, and it works like a native app.

Scan to open EYEWAZ

Point your phone's camera at the code — it opens eyewaz.com/app instantly. No app store needed, nothing to pay, updates arrive automatically.

Set up your device

Two minutes per device — install the app, then switch on the screen-reader features you want.

Android

  1. Scan the QR code (or open eyewaz.com/app in Chrome).
  2. Tap the menu (three dots) then Add to Home screen or Install app.
  3. For full-screen reading, turn on TalkBack: Settings > Accessibility > TalkBack.
  4. To make Urdu your system voice (with our voice engine installed): Settings > Accessibility > Text-to-speech output > EYEWAZ.

iPhone & iPad

  1. Scan the QR code (or open eyewaz.com/app in Safari).
  2. Tap the Share button, then Add to Home Screen.
  3. For system-wide speech, turn on VoiceOver: Settings > Accessibility > VoiceOver.
  4. Tip: say "Turn on VoiceOver" to Siri — the fastest hands-free way.

Computer

  1. Open eyewaz.com/app in Chrome or Edge — that's it.
  2. Optional: click Install in the address bar to add EYEWAZ like a desktop app.
  3. On Windows, our NVDA add-on gives the whole PC an Urdu voice — ask us for the add-on file.

Why don't we flip these switches automatically? Phones treat accessibility settings as protected — no app is allowed to change them silently, and that's a good safety rule. So EYEWAZ does the next best thing: clear spoken guidance, and on Android our app can take you straight to the right settings screen.
