Who we are

We believe the digital world should be open to everyone — including the millions of people who experience it through sound rather than sight.

Our mission

EYEWAZ is a web-based screen reader designed to make the digital world more inclusive for blind and visually impaired users everywhere. With intuitive navigation, high-quality text-to-speech, and seamless compatibility across websites, the app transforms on-screen content into clear, natural audio in real time.

Built with accessibility at its core, it enables users to browse, read, and interact with information independently — turning barriers into opportunities and ensuring that the web is truly for everyone.

What we stand for

Independence

People should be able to read, learn, and decide for themselves — without waiting for help.

Clarity

Natural, human-sounding speech that's easy to follow, in your own language and dialect.

Affordability

Free to start, with cheap, self-hosted voices so cost is never a barrier to access.

Dignity

Technology that adapts to people — not the other way around.

What EYEWAZ does

Photograph printed text, upload documents and PDFs, read web pages, or simply type — and EYEWAZ reads it aloud. Set spoken reminders, keep a personal library you can replay offline, and choose the voice and language that suit you, including Urdu and regional Pakistani dialects.

Who it's for

Anyone who finds reading hard or impossible — people who are blind or have low vision, older readers, people with dyslexia, and anyone who would simply rather listen. EYEWAZ is designed first for the ear, with large controls and full screen-reader support.
