EYEWAZ Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 June 2026. Applies to: EYEWAZ web app and Android app (com.eyewaz.app).

EYEWAZ, operated by WAJD AI, is a free accessibility tool that reads written text aloud for blind and low-vision users. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and your rights.

Android app summary (plain language)
  • The Android app contains no in-app purchases. We never charge through the app.
  • We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.
  • Photos and documents you scan are processed to extract text and are not stored on our servers beyond the moment of processing.
  • You can delete your account and all data at any time.

2. Permissions used by the Android app

PermissionWhy it is needed

MicrophoneVoice commands and the Ask EYEWAZ voice assistant. Only active when you tap the microphone button.
InternetRequired to connect to EYEWAZ services (OCR, translation, speech).

No other permissions are requested. The app does not access contacts, location, storage, or any other sensitive data.

3. No in-app purchases or financial data

The EYEWAZ Android app (com.eyewaz.app) does not process any payments or subscriptions. All billing is handled exclusively through our website in a standard browser. The app never collects, transmits, or stores any financial data, card details, or payment information.

5. Data retention and deletion

  • You can delete your account at any time in the app under Account.
  • Deletion removes all your data from our servers within 30 days.
  • Voice-bank contributors may withdraw consent and we delete the cloned voice on request.
  • Scanned photos and documents are not stored beyond processing.

6. Security

  • All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS.
  • Passwords are stored as bcrypt hashes. We cannot read your password.
  • API keys and secrets are never shipped inside the app or exposed to users.

7. Children

EYEWAZ is not directed at children under 13 (or 16 in the EEA/UK). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your data (GDPR Article 15-20; UK GDPR; CCPA). To exercise these rights or to request account deletion, email us at privacy@eyewaz.com or use the in-app Account section.

9. Changes to this policy

We will update this page if our practices change and update the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
