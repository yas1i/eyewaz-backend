# EYEWAZ voice cloning — speaker consent form

Voice recordings are **biometric personal data**. We only clone a voice with the
speaker's clear, informed, written consent. Keep a signed copy (paper or e-sign).

---

**Speaker name:** ______________________________________________

**Dialect / region:** __________________________________________

**Date:** _______________   **Contact (optional):** _______________

I confirm that:

- The voice in the recording is **my own**, and I am giving this recording freely.
- I consent to EYEWAZ (WAJD AI) creating a **synthetic clone** of my
  voice from this recording, and using it within the EYEWAZ app to read content
  aloud in my dialect for users (including blind and low-vision users).
- I understand the cloned voice can generate speech I did not personally record.
- I can **withdraw consent at any time**, after which EYEWAZ will delete the
  cloned voice and stop using it (existing saved audio may remain with users).
- My recording and cloned voice will be **stored securely**, used only for the
  purpose above, and **not sold** or shared for unrelated uses.
- I am 18 or older (or a parent/guardian is co-signing for a minor).

**Signed:** ______________________________   **Date:** ____________

*(Guardian, if speaker is a minor):* _______________________________

---

### EYEWAZ keeps on record
- This signed form (or its e-signature record).
- Speaker name + dialect (stored with the cloned voice).
- Date/time consent was captured (stored automatically when the voice is created).

### On withdrawal / deletion
Use **Account → Voice bank (admin) → delete** the speaker's dialect voice. This
removes the clone from the provider and the EYEWAZ catalogue.
