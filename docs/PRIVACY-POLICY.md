# EYEWAZ — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-06. Publish this at https://eyewaz.com/privacy and link it
from the app and both store listings. Review with a lawyer before launch; this is
a working draft, not legal advice._

EYEWAZ ("we", WAJD AI) helps blind and low-vision users hear written
text read aloud. This policy explains what we collect and why.

## What we collect
- **Account:** name, email, phone (optional), and a securely hashed password.
- **Content you submit to be read:** photos of text, documents/PDFs, pasted text,
  web links. These are processed to extract and speak the text. Generated audio
  you choose to save lives **on your device** (your library); we don't keep your
  source photos/documents on our servers beyond what's needed to process them.
- **Voice input:** when you use voice commands / Ask EYEWAZ, audio is processed to
  understand your request.
- **Voice-bank recordings (optional, consented):** if a person consents, their
  recording is used to create a synthetic "dialect voice." We store the consent
  record and the resulting voice; we delete both on request.
- **Reminders & preferences:** stored on your device and/or your account.
- **Usage counts:** how many "commands" you've used this month (for plan limits).
- **Biometric unlock (Face ID / fingerprint):** handled by your device; we only
  store a public key for passkey sign-in. **We never receive your biometric data.**

## How it's used
To provide the reading, translation, assistant, reminder, library, and membership
features — nothing else. We do **not** sell your data or use it for advertising.

## Service providers (processors)
We share the minimum needed with: **Microsoft Azure** (OCR, translation,
text-to-speech), **Anthropic** (the assistant), **ElevenLabs** (voice cloning, if
used), **SendGrid** (verification emails), **PayPal / Stripe** (payments — your
card details go to them, never to us), and our hosting/database (Render, MongoDB
Atlas). Each processes data only to provide its part of the service.

## Payments
Subscriptions are processed by PayPal and Stripe. We store your plan and a
subscription reference, not your card details.

## Data retention & deletion
- Saved recordings stay on your device until you delete them.
- You can delete your account and data at any time from the app (Account) or by
  emailing us; we remove your account data and any cloned voice you control.
- Consented voice-bank speakers may withdraw consent; we delete the cloned voice.

## Children
EYEWAZ is not directed at children under 13 (16 in some regions) without a
parent/guardian. Voice-bank speakers must be 18+ or have guardian consent.

## Security
Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS). Secrets are never shipped in the app.
No system is perfectly secure, but we apply reasonable safeguards.

## Your rights
Access, correction, deletion, and (where applicable under GDPR/CCPA) portability
and objection. Contact: **privacy@eyewaz.com**.
